A Little Letter for You

Dear Alya Sofina,

Happy Birthday! 🎉

I hope your special day is filled with happiness, laughter, and beautiful moments. May Allah bless you with good health, success, and endless joy in everything you do.

To be honest, I never expected that meeting someone new could leave such a nice impression on me. From the first time we talked, I noticed how sweet, kind hearted, and genuine you are. Your smile and the way you treat people show a beautiful side of you that is really special.

There is a Latin phrase called Prima Facie, which means "at first sight." Somehow, it reminds me of the feeling I had when I first met you. It was a small moment, but it made me want to know you better.

I know we have only just met, and I do not want to rush anything. I just want you to know that I appreciate your presence, and I enjoy getting to know you. I hope we can create more happy conversations and beautiful memories together.

Stay the kind, sweet, and amazing person you are. May this new year of your life bring you everything you wish for.

Happy Birthday, Alya. 🌸
